Small groups, but big adventures

Our trips are true wilderness journeys where the weather and wildlife dictate the pace and activities of our days. Typically we fly deep into the wilderness and set up a comfortable camp where we expect wildlife to be visible and abundant. Once the tents are up you can leave the camp chores to us and focus on the beauty around you.

Off the beaten path

All of our trips are “off the beaten path”, true wilderness expeditions. We take designing your custom wilderness trip seriously. With decades of experience and exhaustive knowledge of the landscape and wildlife.

Custom trips

The process starts with your desires, the size of your group or family, your expectations, your budget and your abilities. Our role is to advise, inspire, listen, and finally to transform your dreams into reality.

Photography trips

Bring along a sense of adventure and plenty of batteries. All Wild trips have excellent opportunities for wildlife and landscape photography, but a few of them stand out above the rest.

Backpacking trips

Backpacking tours are an opportunity to travel lightly through the wilderness. These guided trips offer a chance to appreciate the landscape step by step, and to experience at your own pace.

Rafting trips

Our river trips are suited for those seeking a wilderness experience rather than an adrenaline rush. On “travel days”, everyone works as a team to load and unload the rafts and maneuver safely down the river.
